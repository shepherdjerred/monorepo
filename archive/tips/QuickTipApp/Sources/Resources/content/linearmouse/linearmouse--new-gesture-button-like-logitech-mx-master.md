- LinearMouse 0.10.3 added a gesture button feature, similar to the Gesture button on the Logitech MX Master series.
- Hold the configured button and move the mouse to trigger different actions based on direction.
- Assign the gesture button in LinearMouse's Buttons settings panel for any supported mouse.
