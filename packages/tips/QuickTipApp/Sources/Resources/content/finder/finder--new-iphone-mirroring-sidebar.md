- In macOS Sequoia, your iPhone appears in the Finder sidebar when connected, letting you sync music, movies, podcasts, books, and more directly from a Finder window.
- Open the iPhone Mirroring app to control your iPhone from your Mac using your keyboard, trackpad, or mouse without touching your phone.
