- Raycast Wrapped 2025 gives you a year-end summary of your productivity stats including Raycast launches, most-used extensions, app usage, AI usage, meeting time, and Focus stats
- All data is stored locally on your Mac — nothing is sent to Raycast servers
- You need at least 14 days of Raycast usage for your Wrapped report to generate
- You can copy your Wrapped summary as an image to share it
