- Click anywhere within an active shell prompt to move the cursor to that position — no more holding Option and clicking.
- This works natively with Fish 4.1+ and Nushell 0.111+ without any extra configuration.
- Powered by Kitty's click_events extension and OSC 133 shell integration.
