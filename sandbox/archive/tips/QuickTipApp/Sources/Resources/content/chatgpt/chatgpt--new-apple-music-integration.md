- ChatGPT has a built-in Apple Music app: connect it in Settings by signing in with your Apple Account, then ask ChatGPT to search for songs, create playlists, or discover new music
- Playlist creation supports highly nuanced requests such as specifying artist gender, time period, instruments, and tempo
- Apple Music subscribers can add generated playlists and songs directly to their library; all users can preview tracks
- Your listening history and existing playlists stay private — ChatGPT can only add to your library, not read from it
- Generated playlists open directly in the Apple Music app once saved
