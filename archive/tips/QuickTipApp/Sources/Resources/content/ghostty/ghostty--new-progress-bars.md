- Ghostty 1.2.0 renders native GUI progress bars when applications emit ConEmu OSC 9;4 escape sequences.
- Progress bars show success and error states visually in the terminal UI, not just in the scrollback.
- Use `progress-style` (added in 1.3.1) to control whether progress bars are displayed at all.
