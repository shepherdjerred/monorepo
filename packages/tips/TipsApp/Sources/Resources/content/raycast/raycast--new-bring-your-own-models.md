- Bring Your Own Models (BYOM) lets you connect any OpenAI-compatible LLM provider to Raycast AI, not just the built-in options
- This is separate from Bring Your Own Key — BYOM supports fully custom providers like self-hosted models or third-party API endpoints
- Enable it in Settings, AI, Experiments, then add your provider's API endpoint and key
- OpenRouter is also natively supported as a custom provider, giving you access to a wide range of models through one API key
