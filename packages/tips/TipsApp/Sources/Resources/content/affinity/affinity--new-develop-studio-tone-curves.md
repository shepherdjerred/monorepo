- The Develop Studio now lets you choose from multiple tone curve modes for RAW editing
- Options include Compressed, Natural, High Contrast, and Log, selectable in Settings
- High Contrast and Log modes are especially useful for images with extreme dynamic ranges
- The selected tone curve mode persists across sessions for a consistent RAW editing baseline
