Glance is a macOS menu bar app that surfaces homelab and SaaS service status via a dashboard window, onboarding flow, and notifications while coordinating background polling. It is implemented in Swift 6 with SwiftUI, AppKit, WidgetKit, and XcodeGen configuration (`project.yml`), and depends on the GRDB Swift package for local persistence. A modular provider architecture boots connectors for Alertmanager, Buildkite, Cloudflare, PagerDuty, and more, sharing data through a GRDB-backed SnapshotStore and a widget extension that uses an app-group entitlement.
