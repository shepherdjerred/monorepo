- ChatGPT now supports third-party app integrations including Spotify, DoorDash, Uber, Canva, Figma, and Expedia — enabling actions like ordering food or booking travel through conversation
- Use the Spotify integration to search music, play songs, and manage your queue by asking ChatGPT in plain language
- Use the DoorDash or Uber Eats integration to browse restaurants, place orders, and track deliveries without leaving ChatGPT
- Use the Expedia integration to search flights, hotels, and activities and get personalized travel recommendations
- Use Canva or Figma integrations to create and edit design assets through natural language prompts in a ChatGPT conversation
