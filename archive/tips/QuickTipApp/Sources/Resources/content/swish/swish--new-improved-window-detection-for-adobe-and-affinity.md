- Swish 1.12 improved window detection for apps that block the Accessibility API, including Adobe and Affinity products.
- Previously these apps could be unresponsive to Swish gestures in certain conditions.
- The fix also improves the speed of the Minimize/Unminimize All menu bar gesture across all apps.
