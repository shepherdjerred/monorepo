- LinearMouse 0.10.3 added process matching so you can target settings at specific running processes.
- This lets you apply custom scroll speed, pointer acceleration, or button mappings to apps that share a bundle ID with other processes.
- Configure process matching in the app-specific section of LinearMouse's settings.
