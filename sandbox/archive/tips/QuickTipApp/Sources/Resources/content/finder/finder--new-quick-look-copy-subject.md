- In macOS Sequoia, you can copy just the subject of a photo in Quick Look: select the file, press Space to open Quick Look, then Control-click the image and choose Copy Subject.
- The subject is separated from the background and copied to your clipboard, ready to paste into a document, email, message, or note.
