- Set a custom background image with `background-image = /path/to/image.png` in your config.
- Control the appearance with `background-image-opacity`, `background-image-position`, `background-image-fit`, and `background-image-repeat`.
- Combine with `background-opacity` to blend the image with your terminal background color.
