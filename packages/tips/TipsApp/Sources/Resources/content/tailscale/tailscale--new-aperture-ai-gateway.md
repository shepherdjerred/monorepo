- Aperture by Tailscale is an AI gateway that sits inside your private network and provides security, visibility, and access controls for LLM sessions and AI agent traffic
- It lets organizations route coding agent and AI tool requests through a centrally managed gateway instead of sending them directly to cloud AI APIs, giving you an audit trail of what models were called and by whom
- Aperture is designed so the secure path for AI access is also the easy path, removing friction from deploying AI tooling safely across an organization
- Currently in alpha, Aperture is accessed through Tailscale and works alongside your existing tailnet policies
