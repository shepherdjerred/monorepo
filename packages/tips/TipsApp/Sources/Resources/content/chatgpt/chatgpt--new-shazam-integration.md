- ChatGPT now includes Shazam integration, letting you identify songs without leaving the app
- Tap the Shazam button in the ChatGPT interface, hold your device near the audio source, and ChatGPT will recognize the song and can answer follow-up questions about it
- After identifying a song, you can immediately ask ChatGPT for information about the artist, album, lyrics, or music recommendations in the same conversation
