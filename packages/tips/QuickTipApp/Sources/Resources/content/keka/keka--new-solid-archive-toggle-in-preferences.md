- Keka 1.6.1 added a Preferences option to enable or disable solid archive creation by default.
- Solid archives compress multiple files together for a better compression ratio, but are slower to extract individual files from.
- Toggle this in Preferences to match your typical use — solid for maximum compression, non-solid for faster random access.
