- The Paste Sequentially command in Clipboard History lets you paste multiple previously copied items one after another into your active app
- Assign a hotkey to Paste Sequentially for the fastest workflow — each press pastes the next item in your clipboard history
- This is useful for repetitive data entry where you need to paste a series of values in order
