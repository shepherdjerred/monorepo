- ChatGPT now offers GPT-5.4, described as the most capable and efficient frontier model for professional work, available to Plus and Pro subscribers
- GPT-5.4 Thinking is a reasoning-focused variant that works through complex problems step by step before answering — useful for math, coding, and multi-step analysis
- GPT-5.4 mini and nano are smaller, faster variants of GPT-5.4 that are now available in ChatGPT for quick, everyday tasks
- GPT-5.3 Instant was introduced specifically to reduce overly dramatic or preachy responses, producing more direct and natural interactions
- Switch between models from the model picker at the top of the chat window to match the model to your task
