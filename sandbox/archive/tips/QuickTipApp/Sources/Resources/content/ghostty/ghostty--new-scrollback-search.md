- Use `Cmd+F` (macOS) or `Ctrl+Shift+F` (Linux/GTK) to search through your terminal scrollback history.
- All matches are highlighted at once — use arrow keys to navigate between them.
- On macOS, drag the search bar to any corner of the window to reposition it.
