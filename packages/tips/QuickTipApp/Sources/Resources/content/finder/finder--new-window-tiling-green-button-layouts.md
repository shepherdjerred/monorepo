- In macOS Sequoia, hover over the green button in the top-left corner of any window to see tiling layout options.
- Choose a layout under Move & Resize to tile the active window into halves or quarters.
- Choose a layout under Fill & Arrange to tile multiple windows side by side at once.
- Hold Option while hovering the green button to see additional layout options.
