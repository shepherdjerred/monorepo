- Safari 18.4 introduced support for Partitioned Cookies (CHIPS), which allows third-party services like embedded widgets to use cookies that are isolated per site rather than shared across the web.
- This improves privacy by preventing cross-site tracking while still letting embedded content remember your preferences on specific sites.
