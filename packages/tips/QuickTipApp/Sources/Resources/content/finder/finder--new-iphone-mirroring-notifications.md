- In macOS Sequoia with iPhone Mirroring, iPhone notifications appear on your Mac with a small iPhone badge so you can tell them apart from Mac notifications.
- Click an iPhone notification on your Mac to jump directly into that app in the iPhone Mirroring window.
- Your iPhone does not need to be nearby for its notifications to appear on your Mac once iPhone Mirroring is set up.
