## Browsing

- `Command-L` - Move focus to the address bar.
- `Command-Shift-\\` - Show all open tabs.
- `Command-Y` - Open browsing history.

## Tabs

- `Command-T` - Open a new tab.
- `Control-Tab` - Move to the next tab.
- `Command-W` - Close the current tab.
