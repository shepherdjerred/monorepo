- ChatGPT Health lets you connect Apple Health, MyFitnessPal, and other fitness apps to get personalized wellness guidance based on your real health data
- Once connected, you can ask ChatGPT to analyze trends in your activity, sleep, heart rate, and other metrics over time
- Set up the connection in the ChatGPT app on iPhone or iPad and your health context is then available in all your conversations
- Use it to ask for personalized nutrition advice, workout recommendations, or help interpreting health readings in plain language
