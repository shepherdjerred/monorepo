- On mobile, you can enable floating navigation buttons that appear over your content so toolbars stay out of the way
- Sliding sidebars let you swipe to reveal the file explorer or right panel without tapping a button
- Full screen mode now automatically hides interface elements so you get an immersive writing experience
- These options are available in the Appearance settings on mobile
