- ChatGPT now offers a Go subscription tier at $8 per month, positioned between the free plan and Plus
- Go subscribers get 10 times more messages per month than the free tier, plus extended memory that persists context across many more conversations
- Go is a good option if you hit free-tier message limits regularly but do not need the full capabilities of Plus or Pro
