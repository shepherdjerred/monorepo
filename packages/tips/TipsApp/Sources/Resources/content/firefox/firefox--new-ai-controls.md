- Firefox 148 added an AI Controls section in Settings where you can manage all AI-enhanced features individually, including translations, alt text in PDFs, tab grouping suggestions, link previews, and the AI chatbot sidebar.
- Toggle "Block AI enhancements" to disable all current and future AI features at once, or pick and choose which ones you want.
- Your AI preferences persist across browser updates once set.
